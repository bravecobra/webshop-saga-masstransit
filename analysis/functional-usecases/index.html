    Functional UseCases

    1. Shopowner creates a Product in the Catalog (Event: NewProductEntered) in Products (=MT-Courier Example -> transactional)
      1. Create a new Product (Event: NewProductCreated) in Catalog
        1. Create a Price for the Product in Pricing
          1. (Event: ProductPriceUpdated) -> Update priceinfo back to Catalog.
        2. Create an StockEntry for the Product in the Warehouse
          1. (Event: StockAmountUpdated) -> updates stockinfo in Catalog.
    2. Shopowner modifies the Price for a Product (Event: ProductPriceRequested) in Pricing
      1. Update Price (Event: ProductPriceUpdated)
        1. Update Product's priceInfo in catalog
    3. Shopowner modifies a StockEntry for a Product (Event: StockAmountRequested) in WareHouse
    4. Update stockamount (Event: StockAmountUpdated) 1. (Event: StockAmountUpdated) -> Update Product's availability in catalog
    5. Customer browses the Catalog viewing products, prices and availability (Query in Products)
    6. Customer manages the content of a ShoppingCart
      1. Add a product and amount to a ShoppingCart (Event: ProductAdded)
        1. Create ShoppingCart if not exists
        2. Add a product to a ShoppingCart
      2. Remove a product from a ShoppingCart (Event: ProductRemoved)
      3. Change amount of a product in a ShoppingCart (Event: ProductAmountChanged)
    7. Customer reviews ShoppingCart details (Query)
      1. Fetch current pricing (optional) (Query in Pricing)
      2. Recheck availability stock (optional) (Query in WareHouse)
    8. Customer submits an SalesTransaction based of current ShoppingCart (Event: ShoppingCartSubmitted) -> MT-Courier
      1. Start new SalesTransaction based on submitted ShoppingCart
      2. Update pricing of SalesTransaction (Event: PriceUpdateRequested) -> IRequest
      3. Set status of ShoppingCart to submitted (Event: SalesTransactionStarted)
        1. Create Delivery in stock with status requested (StockProductRequested)
        2. Create Payment with status unpayed
      4. Set status of SalesTransaction to started
    9. Shopowner reserves the products for a Delivery
      1. Reserve products from WareHouse (Event: StockProductReserved) they still remain in the warehouse. They are marked as sold (linked deliveryId) and are thus available not for purchase.
        1. Update availability amount (Event: ProductStockChanged)
          1. Update catalog availability
      2. Set Delivery to status reserved
    10. Customer provides shipping details for the Delivery (Event: ShippingDetailChanged)
      1. Store shipping details for Delivery
      2. Set Status of Delivery to shippable
        1. Set status of SalesTransaction to InProgress(=reserved/unpayed)
    11. Customer pays for an order (Event: PaymentSubmitted)
      1. Receive payment
      2. Set Payment status to payed
        1. Set SalesTransaction status to purchased (Event SalesTransactionPurchased)
          1. Send notification of purchase to Customer
    12. Customer cancels pending SalesTransaction (Event: SalesTransactionCancelRequested ) -> MT-Courier
      1. Activity Refund in Pricing
      2. Activity Cancel Delivery in Delivery
      3. Set SalesTransaction to status to cancelled
    13. Shopowner starts Delivery (backend Event based on SalesTransactionPurchased)
      1. Checkout from stock and ship Delivery
      2. Set Delivery status to in progress
      3. Set SalesTransaction to status shipped
        1. Send notification of shipment
    14. Customer receives notification of purchase
    15. Shopowner delivers the delivery
      1. Set Delivery to status delivered
        1. Set SalesTransaction to status closed
    16. Customer receives notification of delivery
